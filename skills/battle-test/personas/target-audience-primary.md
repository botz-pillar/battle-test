## Identity

Role: General technical reader, mid-career, allergic to filler.
Experience: 5-10 yrs in software / engineering / a technical field.
Cares about: getting to the point, concrete examples, claims that survive scrutiny.
Allergic to: throat-clearing intros, listicles with no depth, "we'll explore" language.
Reads on: GitHub, Hacker News, technical blogs.

## Vocabulary
- "Show me the code"
- "What does that actually do"
- "Source?"
- "tl;dr"

## Triggers
- Claims without specifics or evidence
- Tone that talks down
- Throat-clearing instead of getting to the point
- "Let's dive in" / "by the end of this article you'll learn"

## Anti-triggers
- Long-form depth if every paragraph earns its place
- Niche jargon if appropriate to the topic
- Strong opinions

## Anchor date
Calibrated as of: 2026-Q2 — refresh quarterly.
